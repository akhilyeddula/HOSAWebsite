Welcome to Middleton HOSA!

HOSA - Future Healthcare Professionals is an international student organization recognized by the U.S. Department of Education, with a mission to promote career opportunities in the health care industry and to enhance the delivery of quality health care to all people Middleton HOSA is one of the largest HOSA chapters in the entire State of Florida.

HOSA provides a unique program of leadership development, motivation, and recognition for students who have interests in pursuing careers in health professions. But above all, it creates a family, where everyone can share the precious moments that come from HOSA. Its members consistently wins numerous awards at regional, state, and international conferences.

Blood Drive - 3/30/21

Signups have already begun for the March 30th blood drive for Brick and Mortar students. Signups will be held during lunch and will continue until the 29th of March.

To donate, you must be at least 16 years old and weigh more than 110 pounds. Students under 18 do need parental consent before donating and must have a physical copy of their form at their appointment.

E-learners who wish to donate can sign up using the link below. The deadline for E-learning students to sign up using the link provided is Monday, March 29th. Once you come to school and donate, you must go home. E-learners are not permitted to stay on campus, for the rest of the day, after they have finished donating.

Please join HOSA in their efforts to make a difference and save lives by signing up to donate!

Parent consent form: https://www.oneblood.org/core/fileparse.php/1168/urlt/parental-permission-v3.pdf

Sign up link: https://donor.oneblood.org/donor/schedules/drive_schedule/947767

If you have any questions, me (@akhilyeddula), Mrs. Dodson (@MrsDodson) or any of the other officers. We hope to see some new faces out there, so make sure you sign up!

Chipotle Fundraiser - 4/3/21

Good evening Middleton HOSA!

We will be having a fundraiser at Chipotle to support HOSA next Saturday on April 3rd from 5-9 pm. The Chipotle location will be 18019 Highwoods Preserve Pkwy in New Tampa and for reference, it is around AMC Highwoods 20.

If you are a resident in the New Tampa area, or even Wesley Chapel, we strongly encourage you to go support Middleton HOSA. There are many things that Chipotle are doing to ensure the safety and wellness of their customers and you can view them in their entirety here (Links to an external site.). To highlight the main points:

  • They are offering contactless delivery and pickup on all digital orders with tamper evident bags (there will be packaging seals on order bags)
  • Follow social distancing and COVID guidelines, such as wearing masks, and constantly sanitizing contact areas
  • Food is prepared in small batches by cooks who are required to wash their hands every 30 minutes at minimum

When ordering, remember that they won't automatically put the proceeds towards us. If you are ordering online for pick-up or contactless delivery, make sure to use promo code 7XR632 before checkout to have it counted towards the fundraiser and get 1 cent off! If you are ordering in-person, should the cashier the flyer on your phone (it will be attached to this announcement) or tell them you are there to support Middleton HOSA. Either option will help support us by providing us 33% of the proceeds. The instructions will also be outlined in greater detail in the attached PDF.

If you have any questions about any part of the process, you can contact me (@akhilyeddula), Suraj (@surajraghunathan) or Mrs. Dodson (@MrsDodson)
