Why Join Us?

Middleton HOSA offers a multitude of opportunities. It is more than just competing in competitions. It is what comes from competiting in those competitions. You are able to foster new friendships, unearth new passions and discover all the different scholarships that HOSA has to offer. Even for those uninitiated in the medical world, HOSA offers a lot even for those who are not well versed in medical sciences. There are many different non-medical events and it still offers many different opportunities that benefit people of all different backgrounds. We are sure that you will find something in HOSA, no matter who you are.
